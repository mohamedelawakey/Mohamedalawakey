I am a third-year Information Systems student at Tanta University, passionate about software development and data science. With a strong technical foundation, I have experience in Python, C++, C#, Java, SQL, PostgreSQL, Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), Data Structures, Git, GitHub, and UI/UX Development.


Before transitioning into the tech industry, I built valuable leadership and communication skills while working as a Sales Manager at PS and later as an Inside Sales Manager at Aliens Club. After three years in sales, I shifted my focus entirely to software development, aligning my career with my academic background.


To deepen my technical expertise, I joined the ITI Summer Training Program in UI Development, where I gained hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript (ES6), Bootstrap, SASS, Photoshop, and Illustrator. As a team leader, I worked on a collaborative project, gaining valuable insights into web development and teamwork under the guidance of experienced mentors.


Seeking further growth, I was accepted into the ALX Data Science Program, where I have been enhancing my problem-solving, decision-making, research, and Agile development skills. Through this program, I also developed proficiency in Google Workspace tools (Docs, Drive, Sheets), Excel, teamwork, and presentation skills.


Alongside ALX, I secured a Data Science Internship at GDG on Campus Al-Azhar, where I am currently advancing my knowledge in statistics and databases. Additionally, I completed the Artificial Intelligence Ambassadors Program by Engineers for a Sustainable Egypt, gaining expertise in Prompt Engineering, AI Ethics, Orange Software, Data Visualization, and Microsoft Power BI.


I am committed to continuous learning and professional growth, striving to make a meaningful impact in software engineering and data science.
